Oil, Gas…and Renewables

At EnPoint Capital, unlike many lenders, we are enthusiastic about oil and gas investing. This is because we understand that while the world is gradually moving away from fossil fuels, they still are a vital bridge to the future. Oil and gas-related companies provide needed products and services, and they can be vibrant and successful. They just need what firms in all industries need: creative, knowledgeable, and responsive capital providers.

We also know that even in the growing renewable-energy sector, it can be difficult for small and midsize firms to obtain the capital they need to execute their plans. This is partly because with renewables, a company’s value proposition and opportunities can be difficult to understand without specialized knowledge.

The people at EnPoint Capital have lived and worked through many commodity cycles and have substantial technical, operational and structuring experience in the sector. If you have a plan that makes sense, and want capital to execute it, talk to us. We’re interested.
